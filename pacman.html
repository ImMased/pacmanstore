HOW TO PLAY

NAVIGATE THE MAZE

Use the arrow keys (or joystick) to move Pac-Man Up, Down, Left, and Right through the winding blue maze corridors.

EAT ALL THE DOTS

Your goal each level is to eat all 240 Pac-Dots scattered throughout the maze. Clear every single one to advance!

AVOID THE GHOSTS

Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde will roam the maze hunting you. One touch from a ghost in normal mode = lose a life.

USE POWER PELLETS

Grab one of the 4 large Power Pellets in the corners to turn all ghosts blue. You can then eat them for bonus points!

GRAB BONUS FRUITS

Bonus fruits appear twice per round under the ghost house. Detour to collect them — they give massive point bonuses.

USE THE TUNNELS

The side tunnels let Pac-Man (and ghosts!) warp to the opposite side. Ghosts slow down here — use it to escape Blinky!

⚡ PRO TIP

The perfect game — eating every dot, fruit, power pellet, and ghost on all 255 levels — earns a maximum score of 3,333,360 points. The game ends at the infamous "kill screen" on level 256 where the screen splits and the game crashes.

EXPLORE THE MAZE

MAZE

THE CLASSIC MAZE

The original Pac-Man maze contains 240 Pac-Dots and 4 Power Pellets, all enclosed within iconic blue walls with two side warp tunnels.

GHOST HOUSE

The central box is the Ghost House — where Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde begin each round before venturing out to hunt Pac-Man.

WARP TUNNELS

The side tunnels on the left and right edges allow instant travel across the screen. Ghosts slow down when inside them — use this to your advantage!

POWER PELLET CORNERS

The 4 large dots at the corners of the maze are Power Pellets. Grab one to turn the tables on every ghost for a limited time.
